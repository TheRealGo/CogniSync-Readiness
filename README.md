# CogniSync-Readiness

[English](README.md) | [日本語](README.jp.md)

**CogniSync-Readiness** is a specialized tool designed to measure and track your **Cognitive Readiness** using two scientifically validated tasks: the **Psychomotor Vigilance Task (PVT)** and the **Flanker Task**.

The primary goal of this project is to help users synchronize their subjective "feeling" of being focused with objective performance data. By measuring foundational alertness (PVT) and executive inhibitory control (Flanker Task), the tool provides a clear, data-driven snapshot of your brain's current state.

## Key Features
- **Precision Measurement**: High-fidelity tracking of reaction times, lapses, and interference control.
- **Data Synchronization**: Log results into a local database to analyze long-term trends and identify your peak performance windows.
- **Self-Calibration**: Compare subjective self-assessments with objective metrics to eliminate cognitive bias and "mental illusions" caused by fatigue or sleep deprivation.

Ideal for anyone requiring high-level mental clarity, **CogniSync-Readiness** serves as a digital "guardrail" to ensure you only perform when your brain is truly ready.
